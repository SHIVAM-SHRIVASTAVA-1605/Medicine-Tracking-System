Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Medicine Reminder ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

Personal Data

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Medical information (medication names, dosages, schedules)
  • Health records and medication history

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address and location data (with permission)

Usage Information

  • Pages or sections accessed
  • Time and date of access
  • Search queries
  • Medication reminder interactions

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site to:

  • Send you medication reminders and notifications
  • Track medication adherence and health patterns
  • Generate personal health reports
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve and optimize our Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary
  • Healthcare Providers: With your consent, we may share information with your healthcare providers
  • Service Providers: With third parties who assist us in operating our Service
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition of assets

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have implemented security features designed to protect your information, the transmission of information via the internet is never completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Contact Us Regarding Your Privacy

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@medicinereminder.com
  • Mailing Address: [Your Company Address]
  • Phone: [Your Phone Number]

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt-out of certain data processing
  • The right to data portability

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your personal data is processed with your explicit consent, and you have the rights outlined in Section 8 above.
